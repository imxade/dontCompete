### Introduction
-----------------

Inorganic chemistry plays a crucial role in chemical engineering, particularly in the design and operation of industrial processes. This note focuses on the key concepts and formulas related to inorganic chemistry, specifically for the GATE CS exam.

### Core Concepts
------------------

#### Group 1 Elements

Group 1 elements are highly reactive alkali metals with one valence electron. They exhibit a strong tendency to lose this electron to form a positive ion (cation).

#### Common Compounds of Group 1 Elements

*   **Sodium Carbonate** ($Na_2CO_3$): A common compound used in various industrial processes, including soap making and glass manufacturing.
*   **Potassium Phosphate** ($K_3PO_4$): Used as a fertilizer and in the production of detergents.

#### Triple Superphosphate

Triple superphosphate is a type of phosphate fertilizer, which contains $P_2O_5$. It is used to increase soil pH and provide phosphorus for plant growth.
